Fantastically well-equipped, the Scrambler 400 X has a class-
beating specification and equipment list. To make it yours,a
choice of more than 25 genuine accessories can be configured
and fitted from new. Designed by the same engineers that
created the Scrambler 400 X, and with the same two-year
unlimited mileage warranty,you can rest assured that each part
will fit perfectly.

STYLE AND COMFORT

Enhance your Scrambler 400 X with direct replacement bullet
indicators. The satin black bodies and machined aluminium
bezels add a touch of class for a subtle new look.

A tank pad offers style and protection to the signature sculpted
tank while rider and pillion comfort for those long rides on tough
terrains can be improved with a genuine accessory quilted seat
that also brings a fresh look, adding yet more premium style to
the Scrambler 400 X.

Plug and play heated grips can be connected easily to the
existing wiring, guaranteeing warm hands all year-round.

  • LED Bullet Indicators
  • Tank Pad
  • Quilted Seat
